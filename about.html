Welcome to the HEALER

WHY DO WE NEED THIS SYSTEM?

At present, we all known that the world is advancing in the direction of modernization but still there are some areas which need deeper attention like the medical department which is a major issue of concern for any being. So to avoid any distress caused to people due to bad management of the medical area, the idea of an online health tech is being introduced which provides relief in all the health related issues so far.

EXISTING SYSTEMS:

There might be many systems present which provides the same ideas like this but the problem that comes is , these websites or applications turns unable to provide all the required features at one place or platform and even if it does it is not managed well.

SOLUTION/IDEA:

The idea is to create something that will provide all the facilities regarding health at just one platform which can be used by a single touch. The system provides financial support , emergency services , professional consult etc. to all the users in an online medium. This idea not only promotes health and well being but also create awareness to stay healthy.

HOW THIS SYSTEMS WILL BE BETTER?

Idea of online Health Card and ID system to help and keep track of a patients treatment history. Introduction of Online Mini-Banks. Direct connection of professional doctors with users. Creating general health awareness. Providing aid in emergency situations and give financial support.
